# Privacy Policy for YouTube Snapshot

**Last Updated:** July 2026

## Overview
YouTube Snapshot ("the Extension") is a tool designed to allow users to capture native-resolution screenshots of YouTube videos. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the Extension.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**We do not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell any of your personal data.**
The Extension operates 100% locally on your machine. All video processing, frame capture, and image downloading happens securely within your browser without communicating with any external servers.

## 2. Permissions Justification
The Extension requests specific permissions in the Chrome Web Store to function correctly. Here is how we use them:
*   `activeTab` and `scripting`: Required to inject the camera icon into the YouTube player and communicate with the video element to capture the frame.
*   `downloads`: Required to save the captured image file directly to your local file system.
*   `offscreen`: Required to securely convert raw image data into a downloadable file while respecting Manifest V3 security restrictions.
*   `storage`: Required to securely save your local preferences (such as your preferred download folder and settings). This data never leaves your computer.

## 3. Analytics and Tracking
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking cookies, or telemetric software. 

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
Because this Extension operates entirely offline and collects no data, we do not anticipate needing to change this policy. However, if minor updates are ever required to comply with future Chrome Web Store developer policies, the updated policy will simply be reflected on this page.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer via the links provided in the extension's popup or the GitHub repository.
